BOARDS: a visualization tool for your inspirations


BOARDS is a digital scrapboard application that lets users capture inspirations on the go and organize them for brainstorming. As a collaboration tool, it provides a connected experience across multiple devices of various scales.

Duration: 4 weeks
Individual Project 
Project Brief: Design a responsive and scalable User Interface that connects the experience across multiple devices ranging from a mobile phone, to digital whiteboards, to interactive kiosks. 
My Role: Ideation, Concept Development, Rapid Prototyping, UX Flow Design, Visual Design

A Connected System -
with Different Functionality Focuses

BOARDS allows capture, modify and view on all three platforms, but each platform is designed to be most useful for a specific functionality.

- Mobile is focused more on capturing contents
- Tablet is focused more on organizing contents and manipulating contents within board
- Board is for viewing content in large scale, which is beneficial for collaborative purposes.


Defining Opportunity Space

BOARDS is a visualization brainstorming tool for creative thinkers. I imagined the persona of this application to be someone who is a maker by job or hobby, who is always looking for inspirations and references for his upcoming design project.

Persona

Storyboard

Ideation began with a set of storyboards of how Stewart might use BOARDS throughout the connected devices. Some interactions have changed since the last few iterations but the concept of connected devices and using gesture to manipulate contents remain the same. 


Interaction Flows

1. Capture Inspirations

BOARDS let you capture any kind of contents into your digital scrapboard. Take a picture of the entire content, or select an area to capture. If it is digital content, BOARDS will automatically save the entire content even if you captured a portion of it.




2. Modify contents

Your mobile phone and tablet can be used to modify contents. The mobile device can be used as a controller for interacting with the larger device, tablet can be used alone for making modifications on your digital scrapboard. The changes made will be reflected to other devices that share the same scrapboard.




3. Collaborate with others

Using BOARDS, you can easily connect multiple devices and work synchronously or asynchronously. The large screen becomes useful for seeing all collaboration occurring real time.
