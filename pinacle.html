PNC PINACLE: New Mobile Experience for Users On-the-go.


Duration: Jan '16 - Aug '16 (On-going)
Team Members: Stephanie Chen, Apoorv Narang, Alan Qui, Andrew Viny
Project Brief: To design new mobile-first experiences that help PNC PINACLE users securely and efficiently manage their cash and make financial decisions on the go.
My Role: User Research, Visual Design, User Experience Design (Currently all members are participating in research)


PINACLE is PNC’s corporate online and mobile banking portal, that helps clients securely and efficiently manage their cash, initiate payments, make financial decisions, and manage their financial risk with a tailored user experience. It is a single unified portal into all of PNC’s corporate banking services for small local businesses to large multinational corporations.

As part of Carnegie Mellon University's MHCI Capstone Project, team Lithium is working with PINACLE Product Management Team to discover and design a feature or set of features for iOS that will help the small and medium business users better manage or provide insight into their daily cash position.



Current Stage

We are currently at our research planning phase, exploring the scope of the problem and identifying which research methods to use in order to gather meaningful data. Once we conduct our research, we will be synthesizing and analyzing our findings to envision design opportunities. We can't share the findings because of NDA, but we will be sharing our research process. Please check back for update!
