Capsl: A Reminder Cap for Your Medicine Bottle


Capsl is a pill reminder service that uses scale technology.  It is a lid (cap) that fits onto the top of typical medicine bottles you get from pharmacies. It blinks green when it is time to take medication. If you don't notice the visual cue, it would send you a notification on your phone. It also alerts you when you try to take more than your prescribed dosage by blinking red.

This project was an idea communication exercise where we were given a technology to design a concept of a product and produce a 2 minute long concept video.

How it Works:
A Simple Idea with Simple Technology


According to research, 58% of adults don't take their medication as recommended.  But it is crucial to take medications on schedule for them to be most effective.

Using simple scale technology that is connected to your mobile phone, Capsl detects whether you have taken  your medication on time and as recommended.  Every medicine bottle  has a bar-code that contains detailed pill information including its weight. Once you scan the bar-code with your mobile app, Capsl is able to detect weight change in your medicine bottle, therefore identify how many pills have been taken out.



How to Use it:
Connected Devices gives you Gentle Reminders

Scan the barcode on your medicine bottle with the mobile app.

Set your medication schedule.

Place Capsl on top of the lid.

Flip it to activate.

Capsl blinks when it's time to take your medication.

You also get a phone notification just in case you missed Capsl blinking.

If you  try to take your medication again because you forgot, Capsl will alert you by blinking red.
